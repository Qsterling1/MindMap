A quick tour of the work I'm most proud of.

The Walking Dead: Survivors was a mobile strategy game I helped launch to thirty million downloads. My Time at Portia is a farming and crafting adventure that grew past ten million copies across every major platform. Its sequel, My Time at Sandrock, ran one of the most successful Kickstarter campaigns in its genre, and one of the townsfolk was even modeled in my likeness.

I supported Clash of Kings, a global hit with over a hundred million players, and Ever Forward, a beautiful puzzle adventure. Superball is a fast, three on three cyberpunk sports game.

I'm also building my own games under 7th Life Studios. Fly and Shadow is an endless runner, and AI Trainer is a turn-based bot battle that teaches how computers and AI actually think.

And I've published two books: Baijiu by Definition, and You're the Problem, Bro: Love Without Responsibility Feels Amazing, Until It Doesn't.
